About us

Welcome to Harshton Junior School-Mbale

Harshton Junior School, is a private mixed Nursery and primary school located in Industrial Division Mbale City Uganda, it is a day and boarding school providing a local curriculum. Harshton Junior school is doing all it takes to measure to the international standards. It’s situated along Ndinya road-Maluku and has an enrollment of over 750 pupils with 38 well-trained teachers and over 35 non-teaching staff members. The pupils come from all over the country and some other parts of the world. The school has been and will continue to be one of the academic hubs of Uganda because of the balanced curriculum offered. We are a multi-cultural institution that provides a well-rounded balanced education to children from different backgrounds.

Mission and Anthem

The school mission is “to provide education that will develop an all-rounded person who is ready to face the challenges of the world.
The school anthem clearly indicates that children, teachers and parents of Harshton Junior School glorify the lord and their prayers ascend on high to achieve school goals and aspirations.

Academics and Discipline

The academic performance is to the required standard and this is because of the well-trained staff members who are talented in handling pupils.
Discipline is a vital element in grooming pupils. It is the key to success as evidenced by the good performance for many consecutive years.

Facilities

The school offers books, pens, pencils crayons, etc.to all pupils in school and beddings to pupils in boarding section.
The meals served in the school are appetizing and each day has its menu which varies with; rice matooke, sweet potatoes, Irish potatoes, cassava (katogo), greens, beans, meat, fruits to mention but a few. Break tea is also served with eats buy Orlistat.
