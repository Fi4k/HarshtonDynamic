The admissions office is the gate way to Harshton Junior School. New children get through the sieve of oral interviews, plus tests in Mathematics and English language.
While our pass mark is 70%, the age and background of the child is taken into account on admission.
To confirm reservation of a place for the child after a successful interview, the Admission/Registration fee should be fully paid. The child is thereby officially admitted into the school.
Parents and visitors are always welcome to be taken around for closer inspection of the school and its facilities.
This office is manned by: The headteacher

Our office is open from 7:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. weekdays and 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. on Saturday throughout the year. You are welcome to register your child at any time during these days.


Requirements for admission on passing the interview.
  • * Admission fee of UGX 20,000 per child
  • * Birth certificate/immunization
  • * Passport copy for foreign pupils
  • * 2 Passport photos of the child
  • * 1 Passport photo of the father
  • * 1 Passport photo of the mother
  • * Report form from Previous school (If applicable)

As administration we nurture a child’s personal growth by providing a friendly and supportive environment.
Our vision being that Nurturing an all-round person to leave a complete life, we labor to facilitate quality education and civilization without reservation. Every child leaves as a successful learner, a confident individual and a responsible citizen.
We do keep parent's and child’s interest at heart.

Harshton Junior School - Mbale

Harshton Junior School, is a private mixed Nursery and primary school located in Industrial Division Mbale City Uganda, it is a day and boarding school providing a local curriculum.
