About us

Welcome to Harshton Junior School-Mbale

Harshton Junior School, is a private mixed Nursery and primary school located in Industrial Division Mbale City Uganda, it is a day and boarding school providing a local curriculum. Harshton Junior school is doing all it takes to measure to the international standards.

Activities

Featured activities

News Writing

Engaging children and encouraging them to write, helping children to develop creativity in story making/telling and leaving them informed on the different events taking place around the world.

Handwriting and Reading

Handwriting and reading are some of the basic skills one must have to attain a better education. The hildren are trained to print using Bic ball point pens and pencils in infant section.

Art and Crafts

The teachers harness the local environment and use the available materials to engage the children in rope making, making ornaments like necklaces, modelling, drawing, painting and many more

Table Tennis

The school offers a complete curriculum whereby games and sports play an important part. Table tennis lessons going on and each class timetable has periods for physical education and games.Each class timetable has periods for physical education and games.

Day Care

Day care is a special program to cater for children between one and three years whose parents are not able to stay with them before mid-day. These are collected in the afternoon (i.e. from 12pm-1pm).

Preparations For PLE

Candidates having a meeting with teachers. P7 pupils usually hold meetings with their teachers especially towards their final exams. These meetings are aimed at finding ways in which the children's performance can be improved. The last few weeks to the Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) are crucial and the teachers as well as administrators need to ensure that the students are comfortable and have all that is necessary to get the best results.

Plans For The Near Future

  • COMPUTER SKILLS

    We are planning in such a way that every child who goes through Harshton Junior School leaves with basic skills and knowledge of the computer. Computer lessons shall be offered by the school right from pre-primary to primary seven.
    The school computer laboratory shall be well stocked with modern computers and instructors who will ably conduct lessons with the best facilitation. This will give pupils a competitive advantage when they join secondary schools where computer knowledge is examinable at the national level.

  • Cookery

    The school has a near future plan to ensure that Learners aged 10-12 years are taught all methods of cooking such as: baking, roasting, frying, steaming, simmering and others. They will be able to learn how to prepare different types of dishes some which are prepared for commercial purposes or class projects.

  • Tailoring

    The school is further planning to instill tailoring skills in learners. Tailoring involves needle work where learners will use their hands and sewing machines to draft different patterns depending on what item is to be made. They will learn to make sanitary towels, dresses, aprons, skirts, petty coats, sweaters and many others.
    Apart from sewing or mending their clothes, they will gain knowledge of how to be self-reliant. These skills will prepare children to be future entrepreneurs as they will acquire income generating skills.
